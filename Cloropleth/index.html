Race Disparity in Canada's Offender Population

A look at Correctional Services Canada's (CSC) offender profiles (2012-2018)

Race Disparity Index by Province

This map visualizes race disparity of Aboriginal and Black Canadian offenders. A Race Disparity Index score was calculated for each province by comparing the race of the 2016 offender population with the general (Source: StatCan 2016) provincial population.

Click on each province or territory to view a summary of index scores by race group.
Use the selector at the top right corner of the map to view the Aboriginal or Black Race Disparity Index score.
